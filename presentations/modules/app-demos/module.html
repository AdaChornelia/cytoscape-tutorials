NDEx

NDEx provides network storage, sharing, distribution, and publication for the Cytoscape ecosystem. Networks in NDEx can range from a few edges to over 10 million.

NDEx

Cytoscape users can find and download networks from NDEx. They can save networks to their NDEx account.

NDEx

NDEx is a FAIR data commons where organizations and individual authors can publish networks at stable URLs and DOIs.

NDEx

  • NDEx is a:
    • Public resource of networks
    • Publication platform for authors and organizations
    • "Dropbox for Networks"
    • Network hub for Cytoscape and other applications
